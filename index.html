Depth Jumps are great for building resistant knees as well as jumping power, by jumping down from a challenging height and then immediately back up.

What is a Depth Jump?
Depth Jumps are great for building resistant knees as well as jumping power, by jumping down from a challenging height and then immediately back up.

How do I perform a Depth Jump?
In the Depth Jump you look for a height from which you dare to jump without hurting yourself(Step 1 on picture). From the box you take a step forward into the air and drop (Step 2). As soon as you get on the ground you try to resist the momentum and jump as high as you possibly can (Step 3 and 4).
